mother, mother, please stop fumbling over your words, i am tired of sanding down the sharp edges of your accent.⁣

⁣ ingrate, ingrate, your tongue melts like butter in your mouth, may the words of my own country choke you.⁣


intaglio



like the skin of a pineapple,
i guard and keep you at bay,
but the knife that slices in,
reveals beneath,
flesh
that is sour,
that is sweet,

and i for one cannot tell
whether this brings me joy or pain,

joy from the loss of a burden,

or pain from an open wound.
